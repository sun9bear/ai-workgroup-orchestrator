# Code of Conduct / 行为准则

## Our Standard

We want this project to be a practical, respectful place to build safer AI-assisted development workflows.

Expected behavior:

- Be direct, kind, and specific.
- Critique ideas and code, not people.
- Assume good intent, but verify technical claims with evidence.
- Respect privacy and do not request or publish secrets.
- Avoid pressuring maintainers or contributors to enable unsafe automation shortcuts.

## 我们的标准

我们希望这个项目成为一个务实、尊重彼此、共同构建更安全 AI 编程协作机制的地方。

期望行为：

- 直接、友善、具体。
- 批评想法和代码，不攻击个人。
- 默认善意，但技术判断要用证据验证。
- 尊重隐私，不索要或发布密钥。
- 不催促维护者或贡献者绕过安全 gate 开启危险自动化。

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private data, credentials, logs, or business information without consent.
- Encouraging unsafe deployment, credential, production, or automation actions.
- Repeatedly ignoring maintainer safety boundaries.

## 不可接受行为

- 骚扰、侮辱、威胁或歧视性表达。
- 未经同意发布私有数据、凭据、日志或业务信息。
- 鼓励不安全的部署、凭据、生产环境或自动化操作。
- 反复无视维护者设定的安全边界。

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove content, close issues, block users, or restrict participation when needed to keep the project safe and useful.

维护者可以在必要时删除内容、关闭 issue、屏蔽用户或限制参与，以保持项目安全和有用。
